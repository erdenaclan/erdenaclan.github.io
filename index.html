Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to both Narrat AI and VocabVine and describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1 Overview

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our applications, how we use that information, and your rights regarding your data. By using Narrat AI or VocabVine, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about how you interact with the app (e.g., features used, session duration) to improve our services.
  • Device Information: General device type, operating system version, and language settings to ensure compatibility.
  • User-Generated Content (Narrat AI): Journal entries and voice recordings you create are stored locally on your device and/or encrypted cloud storage. We do not read or sell your personal journal content.
  • Vocabulary Progress (VocabVine): Your learning history and progress data to power personalized recommendations. This data is stored locally and may be synced anonymously.
  • Ad Interaction Data (VocabVine): If you interact with optional ads, basic interaction data is collected by our ad partners in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving app functionality and user experience.
  • Personalizing content and learning recommendations.
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical issues.
  • Analyzing anonymous usage trends to guide future development.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

4 Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. User-generated content in Narrat AI is treated as private and is never used for training AI models without explicit consent. We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services.

5 Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following types of third-party services to operate:

  • AI Processing: Anonymous or encrypted data may be sent to AI inference services to power features. No personally identifiable information is shared.
  • Analytics: Anonymous crash reporting and usage analytics to maintain app stability.
  • Advertising (VocabVine): Optional ad networks may collect interaction data subject to their own privacy policies.

We carefully select partners that uphold high privacy standards and do not allow them to use your data for purposes beyond the services they provide to us.

6 Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at aclanerdenajshhd@icloud.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at aclanerdenajshhd@icloud.com.

8 Data Deletion

You may delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the application. For cloud-synced data, please contact us at aclanerdenajshhd@icloud.com with your deletion request and we will process it within 30 days.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our apps after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at: aclanerdenajshhd@icloud.com. We will respond within 5 business days.
